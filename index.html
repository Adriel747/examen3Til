🏄 Welcome to Surf City — Santa Cruz, California!

📍 Santa Cruz · California · USA

Santa Cruz is a cool city in California. It's on the Pacific Ocean. There are lots of amazing hangouts here! There's a famous skatepark called Vans Off the Wall. It's between the beach and the movie theater. There are also 200 restaurants and coffee shops near the beach. The coffee shop next to the skatepark is called "Wave Brew" — it's really popular!

In the center of Santa Cruz, there's a big mall with 85 stores. Across from the mall, there's a park called Harvey West Park. It's great for runners and families. There isn't a gym in the park, but there's one next to the movie theater.

My name is Jake and I live in Santa Cruz. My bedroom is really cool. My surfboard is next to the closet. My tablet and my speakers are on the desk. My sneakers are always under the bed! This is my favorite city — I love it here.
